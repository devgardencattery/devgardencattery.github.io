Welcome to Garden Cattery


Our cat chloe

Welcome to Garden Cattery, home to myself Susan and husband Shane along with Chloe our own cat. The cattery is situated in our back garden and is perfect for us to be able to keep an eye on all our boarders whilst they play, snooze or just watch the world go by. The pens are in a courtyard setting with bird feeders and a water feature to also keep the cats amused and interested during their stay.

The cattery has been built to the Feline Advisory Bureau Guidelines and the comfort and wellbeing of our boarding cats is paramount to us.
