Directions

Garden Cattery is 13 miles south of Norwich.

From the A11:

  • Take the B1172 exit toward Wymondham/Besthorpe/Spooner Row
  • Head towards Spooner Row across both roundabouts
  • After crossing the railway track turn right at the T-junction
  • Follow the road to Bunwell for about 3.5miles, we are on the right hand-side (before the road Greenways on the right)
