Rates

We have 25 pens, including 4 double pens and 1 triple pen. The triple pen joins onto one of the doubles, which can be opened up into a pen for 5 cats. These are the daily boarding rates:

Pen Combination 1 Single 1 Double 1 Triple 2 Doubles 1 Double

  • Rates are for cats from the same household sharing.
  • Because of the intensive regime of cleaning, disinfection and preparation required before and after each stay, our daily rate is charged for the day of delivery and the day of collection.
  • Balance of accounts must be settled at the time of collection and payment by cash or cheque is accepted.
  • Owners are responsible for any veterinary bills that are incurred during their cats stay and must be settled on collection. Please advise us if your cat is insured.
