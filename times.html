Rates

The following table shows the boarding rates per day:

1 cat 2 cats 3 cats 4 cats
£8.50 £13.50 £17.50 £20.50

  • Rates are for cats from the same household sharing. Special rates are given to larger families and long stays. Up to five cats can share our family accommodation.
  • Because of the intensive regime of cleaning, disinfection and preparation required before and after each stay, our daily rate is charged for the day of delivery and the day of collection.
  • Balance of accounts must be settled at the time of collection and payment by cash or cheque is accepted.
  • Owners are responsible for any veterinary bills that are incurred during their cats stay and must be settled on collection. Please advise us if your cat is insured.

Christmas and New Year

As well as the Christmas and New Year bank holidays, we are also closed for boarders checking in and out on the 27th December and on New Year’s day. We do not increase our daily price over Christmas or New Year period.
